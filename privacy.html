Privacy

Last updated 5 June 2026

The short version: this site collects nothing about you. No cookies, no analytics, no third-party requests — and the converter runs entirely in your browser.

Nothing you type leaves your browser. The live converter and the documentation viewer run as plain JavaScript on the page you loaded. The timestamps you encode or decode are processed locally and are never transmitted, logged, or stored anywhere.

What we collect

Nothing. There are no accounts, no sign-up, no contact forms, and no analytics. We do not build a profile of you and we have nothing to sell or share.

Cookies and local storage

None. The site sets no cookies and writes nothing to your browser’s local storage or anywhere else on your device.

Third-party requests

None. Everything the page needs — the code, the fonts, the documentation — is served from this site’s own domain. There are no content-delivery networks, no font services, no trackers, no embedded widgets, and no advertising. Loading the site contacts no one but our own web host.

Server logs

Like virtually every web server, our hosting provider may record standard access-log entries when a file is requested — typically your IP address, the time, the file requested, and your browser’s user-agent string. These logs exist for normal site operation and security, are not used to track or identify you, and are not combined with any other data. We do not add anything beyond what ordinary web hosting produces.

Links out

Pages here link to external sites such as GitHub. Once you follow such a link, the privacy practices of that site apply — not this one.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the “last updated” date above changes with it. Given how little the site does, substantive changes are unlikely.
